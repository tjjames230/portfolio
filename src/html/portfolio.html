myLibrary

myLibrary is a user-friendly application designed to help users track and manage their personal collection of books and articles. The application provides an intuitive interface for adding, organizing, and accessing a digital library. Users can track their reading progress for the day, week and month, helping them set and achieve their goals.

Coffee Time

Coffee Time is a vibrant social platform designed to bring together coffee enthusiasts and local coffee shops. The application allows users to share their favorite drinks, discover special deals, and connect with like-minded individuals. Coffee Time creates a community around the love of coffee, tea and other drinks, while promoting local businesses.
